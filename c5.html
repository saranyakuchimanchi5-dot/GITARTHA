✨Karma Sanyasa Yoga✨

🌿 Act without selfishness — that’s true renunciation.

💫Sloka of Karma Sanyasa Yoga

श्रीभगवानुवाच
सन्न्यासः कर्मयोगश्च निःश्रेयसकरावुभौ।
तयोस्तु कर्मसंन्यासात्कर्मयोगो विशिष्यते॥

🌿Moral of Karma Sanyasa Yoga

True renunciation is not about leaving the world,
but doing your duties without attachment.
Let go of ego and act with purpose.
