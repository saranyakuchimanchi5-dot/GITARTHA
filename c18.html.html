✨Moksha Sannyasa Yoga✨

☸ This final chapter Guides on how renunciation and selfless action lead to ultimate freedom – Moksha.

📜💫 Sloka of Moksha Sannyasa Yoga

श्रीभगवानुवाच:
सर्वधर्मान्परित्यज्य मामेकं शरणं व्रज।
अहं त्वां सर्वपापेभ्यो मोक्षयिष्यामि मा शुचः॥

🌿🪷 Moral of Moksha Sannyasa Yoga

Surrender to the Divine completely,
and be free from all burdens – this is the path to liberation.
