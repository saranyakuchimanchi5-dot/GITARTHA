✨Kshetra Kshetrajna Vibhaga Yoga✨

🌟 The Difference Between The Body (Kshetra) And The Knower Of The Body (Kshetrajna).

📜💫 Sloka of Kshetra Kshetrajna Yoga

🕉 श्रीभगवानुवाच:
इदं शरीरं कौन्तेय क्षेत्रमित्यभिधीयते।
एतद्यो वेत्ति तं प्राहुः क्षेत्रज्ञ इति तद्विदः॥

🌿🪷 Moral of Kshetra Kshetrajna Yoga

Understand the body is temporary, but the soul is eternal.
Real wisdom lies in knowing the difference.
