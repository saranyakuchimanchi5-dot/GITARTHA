🕉✨ Chapter 8: Aksara Brahma Yoga ✨🌌

🌠 The Soul Is Eternal, Never Dies.

📜💫 Sloka of Aksara Brahma Yoga

श्रीभगवानुवाच:
अक्षरं ब्रह्म परमं स्वभावोऽध्यात्ममुच्यते।
भूतभावोद्भवकरो विसर्ग: कर्मसंज्ञित:॥

🪷 Moral of Aksara Brahma Yoga

Understanding the eternal soul leads to freedom from fear of death.
Sincere devotion and remembrance of the Divine at the time of death ensures liberation.
