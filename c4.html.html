✨Jnana Karma Sanyasa Yoga✨

🧘🏻Path of wisdom and action

💫Sloka of Jnana Karma Sanyasa Yoga

यदा यदा हि धर्मस्य ग्लानिर्भवति भारत ।
अभ्युत्थानम् अधर्मस्य तदात्मानं सृजाम्यहम्॥

🌿Moral of Jnana Karma Sanyasa Yoga

True wisdom leads to freedom.
Work with knowledge, not attachment.
Let go of ego and act with purpose.
