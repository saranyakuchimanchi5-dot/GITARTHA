✨Bhakti Yoga✨

💖 The greatness of devotion and the qualities of a true devotee.

📜💫 Sloka of Bhakti Yoga

श्रीभगवानुवाच:
ये तु सर्वाणि कर्माणि मयि संन्यस्य मत्पराः।
अनन्येनैव योगेन मां ध्यायन्त उपासते॥

🌿🪷 Moral of Bhakti Yoga

Pure love and devotion towards God bring peace and liberation.
A devotee sees the Lord in all and serves with humility.
