✨ Vibhuti Yoga✨

🌟Krishna’s Divine powers (vibhutis) present in all creation.

📜💫 Sloka of Vibhuti Yoga

श्रीभगवानुवाच:
अहमात्मा गुडाकेश सर्वभूताशयस्थित:।
अहमादिश्च मध्यं च भूतानामन्त एव च॥

🌿🪷 Moral of Vibhuti Yoga

God is present in all greatness and beauty.
Seeing His divine presence in everything brings deep respect and devotion.
