✨ Dhyana Yoga ✨

🌺 Inner peace is found through focused meditation and balance in life.

📜💫 Sloka of Dhyana Yoga

श्रीभगवानुवाच:
यदा विनियतं चित्तमात्मन्येवावतिष्ठते।
निःस्पृहः सर्वकामेभ्यो युक्त इत्युच्यते तदा॥

🌿🪷 Moral of Dhyana Yoga

A disciplined mind leads to true happiness.
Dhyana (meditation) connects you with your inner self,
bringing peace, clarity, and spiritual progress.
