✨Raja Vidya Raja Guhya Yoga ✨

🔐Devotion Is The Highest Path To Reach God.

📜💫 Sloka of Raja Vidya Raja Guhya Yoga

श्रीभगवानुवाच:
राजविद्या राजगुह्यं पवित्रमिदमुत्तमम्।
प्रत्यक्षावगमं धर्म्यं सुसुखं कर्तुमव्ययम्॥

🌿🪷 Moral of Raja Vidya Raja Guhya Yoga

The purest knowledge is devotion.
Loving and trusting God brings joy, peace, and eternal connection.
