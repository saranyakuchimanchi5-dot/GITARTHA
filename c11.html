✨Vishwarupa Darshana Yoga✨

🔱Krishna shows Arjuna His cosmic (Vishwarupa) form – infinite and divine.

📜💫 Sloka of Vishwarupa Darshana Yoga

श्रीभगवानुवाच:
पश्य मे पार्थ रूपाणि शतशोऽथ सहस्रश:।
नानाविधानि दिव्यानि नानावर्णाकृतीनि च॥

🌿🪷 Moral of Vishwarupa Darshana Yoga

God exists beyond human limits — in all forms and time.
Seeing His universal form teaches us humility and surrender.
