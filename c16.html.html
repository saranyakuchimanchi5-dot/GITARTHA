✨ Daivasura Sampad Vibhaga Yoga ✨

🌟The divine (daivi) and demoniac (asuri) qualities in human nature.

📜💫 Sloka of Daivasura Sampad Vibhaga Yoga

श्रीभगवानुवाच:
अभयं सत्त्वसंशुद्धिर्ज्ञानयोगव्यवस्थितिः।
दानं दमश्च यज्ञश्च स्वाध्यायस्तप आर्जवम्॥

🌿🪷 Moral of Daivasura Sampad Vibhaga Yoga

Divine qualities lead to liberation, while negative traits cause bondage.
Cultivate virtues like fearlessness, truth, and compassion.
