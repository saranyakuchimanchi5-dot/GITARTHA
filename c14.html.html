✨Gunatraya Vibhaga Yoga ✨

🔄 The Three Qualities – Sattva (goodness), Rajas (passion), and Tamas (ignorance) – That Influence Human Behavior.

📜💫 Sloka of Gunatraya Vibhaga Yoga

श्रीभगवानुवाच:
त्रिभिर्गुणमयैर्भावैरेभिः सर्वमिदं जगत्।
मोहितं नाभिजानाति मामेभ्यः परमव्ययम्॥

🌿🪷 Moral of Gunatraya Vibhaga Yoga

⚖ Balance the three gunas to rise above them.
True freedom comes when you go beyond all qualities.
