✨ Purushottama Yoga ✨

🌿 This chapter describes the eternal tree of life and the Supreme Self beyond both body and mind.

📜💫 Sloka of Purushottama Yoga

श्रीभगवानुवाच:
ऊर्ध्वमूलमध:शाखमश्वत्थं प्राहुरव्ययम्।
छन्दांसि यस्य पर्णानि यस्तं वेद स वेदवित्॥

🌿🪷 Moral of Purushottama Yoga

Know the root of all life is divine.
Realizing the Supreme Self is the path to liberation and inner mastery.
