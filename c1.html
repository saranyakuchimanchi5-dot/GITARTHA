🕉The Yoga Of Arjuna's Grief

💫Sloka of Arjuna Vishada Yoga

धृतराष्ट्र उवाच:
धर्मक्षेत्रे कुरुक्षेत्रे समवेता युयुत्सवः।
मामकाः पाण्डवाश्चैव किमकुर्वत
मा कर्मफलहेतुर्भूर्मा ते सङ्गोऽस्त्वकर्मणि॥

🌿Moral of Arjuna Vishada Yoga

Even the strongest minds can become
weak in times of emotional conflict.
True strength lies in seeking guidance and
choosing the right path over personal attachments.
