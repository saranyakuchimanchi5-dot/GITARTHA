✨ Jnana Vijnana Yoga ✨

🌟 True wisdom and scientific knowledge lead to knowing the Supreme.

📜💫 Sloka of Jnana Vijnana Yoga

श्रीभगवानुवाच:
मय्यासक्तमना: पार्थ योगं युञ्जन्मदाश्रय:।
असंशयं समग्रं मां यथा ज्ञास्यसि तच्छृणु॥

🪷 Moral of Jnana Vijnana Yoga

Knowledge (Jnana) and realization (Vijnana) together help one understand the true nature of God.
Devotion, awareness, and trust in the Divine bring spiritual wisdom and peace.
