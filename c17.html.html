✨Shraddhatraya Vibhaga Yoga ✨

🪔The three types of faith (shraddha) based on one's nature.

📜💫 Sloka of Shraddhatraya Vibhaga Yoga

श्रीभगवानुवाच:
त्रिविधा भवति श्रद्धा देहिनां सा स्वभावजा।
सात्त्विकी राजसी चैव तामसी चेति तां शृणु॥

🌿🪷 Moral of Shraddhatraya Vibhaga Yoga

Faith is shaped by one's qualities.
Sattvic faith leads to peace, while tamasic misleads. Choose wisely.
